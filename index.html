    1.Folding Bed :
  • 4 Members in a Team ,My Role as a Captain and Designer in the Team.
  • The design and fabrication of multispecialty hospital use folding beds play a vital role in providing comfort, safety, and functionality to patients and healthcare providers. This abstract presents an overview of the key aspects involved in the design and fabrication process of these beds, highlighting their importance in the healthcare industry.The primary objective of this study is to develop folding beds that meet the specific requirements of a multispecialty hospital setting. The research focuses on incorporating features that enhance ergonomics, adjustability, and support mechanisms to ensure optimal patient comfort and safety. The beds are designed to provide ease of use for both patients and healthcare providers, considering accessibility and mobility within the hospital environment. The selection of suitable materials is crucial to ensure durability and weightbearing capacity of the folding beds. This study explores different materials and their properties to identify the most suitable options that can withstand the demands of hospital use while maintaining structural integrity and longevity.
